Welcome to Spotify Annotator!

If you've ever wanted to add notes at various points in a playlist, this tool is for you. It works by adding local songs with custom names — Spotify doesn't try to check if the songs on your computer are real, so they can have any name you want.

To get started, simply highlight all of the songs in a playlist on the desktop app, then paste them into the big friendly box on the left.

Then over on this side you'll see an editable representation of your playlist, letting you insert new fake songs into a playlist.

Once you're done, just copy and paste the text on the left back into a playlist.
